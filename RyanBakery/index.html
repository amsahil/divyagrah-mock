Our Story

More than a bakery — it's a labour of love

Renu Bakery started as a passion for creating beautiful, delicious cakes from home. Based in Silchar, Assam, we've grown into one of the most loved local bakeries with over 4,550 happy orders — all completely eggfree.

🥚

100% Eggfree

Every single cake and treat we make is completely eggfree — perfect for vegetarian families and dietary preferences.

🏠

Made at Home, with Care

No factory. No bulk production. Each cake is baked fresh in our home kitchen with handpicked quality ingredients.

🎨

Fully Customizable

Send us a reference image, a theme, or just an idea — and we'll turn it into a cake that exceeds your expectations.
